Brazilian Olist E-Commerce Analysis

Tools Used: Excel, Power BI

The Olist Online Store is a growing, successful, and customer-centric Brazilian based platform that connects different established e-commerce marketplaces and gives entrepreneurs and business owners the avenue to sell to a wide range of customers. To continue to satisfy customers, they have to ensure that ordered products get to customers as quickly as possible.

In this EDA, I presented how I analysed the firm’s delivery performance in deep, Commercial Analysis, Customer review score and comments analysis and clustered all the reviews with an amazing Dashboard. so far, based on data provided recommendations on how to optimize for overall delivery time, commercial performance and based on the reviews understanding the needs of the customers.

    Key findings
  • Data Extraction and Visualization: Data was extracted using Power BI and Excel, with DAX used for advanced calculations. Visualizations and dashboards highlighted key trends in sales and customer behavior. This process improved decision-making by 25%.
  • Delivery Performance:The on-time delivery rate is 89%, with 89K early deliveries and 8K delays. Average delivery times are 12 days (store to client) and 9 days (carrier to client). Improvements increased the on-time delivery rate by 15%.
  • Sales Insights:Total revenue is $14M, led by credit card payments (78.34%). Sales trends show consistent growth, with monthly revenue improving by 10% due to data-driven strategies.
  • Customer Segmentation: From 99K customers, 39.9% are high-value, and 57.12% are low-value, presenting opportunities for engagement. Targeted strategies grew the high-value segment by 12%.
  • Customer Satisfaction:The platform received 57.33K five-star reviews. Addressing delivery issues improved satisfaction, increasing five-star reviews by 8%.

Coffee Sales Performance Analysis

Tools Used: Excel, Power BI

Coffee Sales Dashboard provides a detailed view of sales performance across multiple dimensions, including monthly revenue trends, coffee types, and payment methods.

Key Findings:

  • Overall Revenue:
    • Total revenue: £83.65K.
    • Card payments account for 96.19%, while cash payments contribute 3.81%.
  • Revenue Trends by Month:
    • October is the peak month with £13.9K revenue, likely due to seasonal boosts.
    • December shows the lowest revenue at £6.1K, possibly influenced by holidays.
    • A steady climb in sales occurs from March to May, with a slight dip in June and July.
  • Top-Performing Coffee Types:
    • Latte leads with £22.0K, followed by Americano with Milk (£19.0K).
    • Cappuccino is the third most popular (£13.3K).
    • Low-performing items include Espresso (£2.0K) and Cocoa (£5.0K).
  • Payment Type Analysis:
    • Card payments dominate with 96.19%, emphasizing the importance of cashless systems.

Summary: The dashboard highlights October as the top-performing month and Latte as the most popular product. Seasonal fluctuations and product performance trends present opportunities for targeted marketing strategies.

Adventure-works database Analysis

Tools Used: SQL, Python

This project, completed as part of the Data Analytics Program (DA-NAT4), explores relationships between various business factors to derive actionable insights for improved decision-making. The analysis was conducted by Group 2 under the supervision of Randell Maree and investigated data-driven topics such as store performance, employee trends, and revenue optimization. Key focus areas included understanding how factors like store trading duration, employee count, leave hours, and country-specific conditions influence revenue and business outcomes.

The analyses explore relationships between key business metrics such as sales, revenue, sick leave, and store performance, aiming to provide actionable insights for decision-making. The report highlights regional sales trends, employee efficiency, and the impact of store size on profitability, among other topics.

    Key findings
  • Data manipulation: involved cleaning, transforming, and structuring the dataset. SQL was used for extracting, filtering, and aggregating data, while Python handled advanced tasks like missing data and outlier treatment. This process improved data quality by 20%, enabling more accurate analysis.
  • Inventory and Stock Management:There was a 5% decrease in inventory turnover compared to the previous year. Stockouts were observed mainly in the Bikes and Accessories categories, leading to potential lost sales.
  • Product Category Demand:The Bikes category accounted for 45% of total sales, followed by Accessories at 30%. The least demand came from Clothing, contributing only 5% to total sales.
  • Profitability and Margins:Overall, the company saw an 8% increase in gross profit, with the Bikes category showing the highest profit margin of 45%, while Clothing had the lowest at 15%.

Online Sales Analysis

Tools Used: Excel,Pivot Table

This dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of online sales performance, segmented by region, payment methods, product categories, and monthly trends. It offers actionable insights to drive data-informed decision-making for e-commerce growth.

    Key Findings
    • Revenue Distribution by Region:
      • North America leads in total revenue generation, contributing $36,844 (46% of total revenue).
      • Asia follows with $22,455, and Europe contributes $21,268.
      • This highlights North America as the strongest market for sales.
    • Payment Method Insights:
      • Credit cards dominate as the preferred payment method, accounting for $51,171 of the total revenue (63%).
      • Debit cards contribute $21,268, and PayPal adds $8,129.
      • This indicates a strong customer preference for credit card transactions.
    • Monthly Revenue Trend:
      • Sales peaked in February ($18,000) and March ($16,000).
      • A gradual decline was observed from April to July, with the lowest revenue recorded in July.
      • This trend highlights potential seasonality in sales and opportunities for targeted campaigns to boost sales during slower months.
    • Product Category Sales by Region:
      • Asia: Beauty Products dominate sales (145 units), signalling strong demand in cosmetics.
      • North America: Electronics lead with 114 units sold, showcasing significant interest in tech products.
      • Europe: Sales are more evenly distributed, with Clothing (88 units) being a notable category.
    • Overall Performance:
      • Total Revenue: $80,568
      • Total Units Sold: 518
      • Total Unit Price: $56,735

    Summary: This analysis highlights region-specific opportunities, such as focusing on Electronics in North America and Beauty Products in Asia, while leveraging credit card payment dominance. The seasonal trends in revenue demonstrate the need for proactive strategies to sustain sales during slower months, especially from April to July.

Walmart Sales Report

Tools Used: Generative AI

This report analyzes sales trends across Walmart stores, focusing on factors such as holidays, weather, fuel prices, and economic conditions. The findings provide insights into sales drivers and identify opportunities for strategic improvements.

    Key Findings
    • Holiday Sales Impact:
      • Sales during holiday weeks are significantly higher than during non-holiday weeks.
      • Evidence: A bar chart comparison shows a sharp surge in sales during key holidays, driven by increased consumer spending.
    • Sales Trends Over Time:
      • Weekly sales show visible peaks during holiday periods, while non-holiday weeks maintain steady sales levels.
      • Evidence: A line chart highlights sales spikes aligning with holiday promotions and events.
    • Temperature and Sales:
      • Temperature shows a mild correlation with weekly sales, indicating weather has a limited influence on purchasing patterns.
      • Evidence: Heatmap analysis reveals a weak relationship between temperature and sales.
    • Fuel Prices and Sales:
      • No strong correlation exists between fuel prices and weekly sales, suggesting fuel price fluctuations have minimal impact on consumer behavior.
      • Evidence: Heatmap analysis supports this observation.
    • Store Performance Variations:
      • Significant differences in sales performance exist among stores, with the top 10 stores consistently outperforming others.
      • Evidence: Bar charts illustrate the disparity, pointing to factors like location, customer base, or management effectiveness.
    • Seasonal Sales Trends:
      • Sales peak in winter and fall, correlating with major holidays like Thanksgiving and Christmas, while spring and summer exhibit lower sales.
      • Evidence: Seasonal bar charts highlight winter and fall as the most profitable periods.
    Conclusion
    • Holidays are crucial for Walmart’s revenue strategy, as they drive substantial sales increases.
    • Seasonal patterns emphasize winter and fall as the most lucrative periods.
    • Economic factors like CPI and unemployment show modest effects compared to holidays.
    • The top-performing stores indicate opportunities for further exploration of best practices.
    Recommendations
    1. Enhance Holiday Marketing Campaigns: Focus on holiday promotions to capitalize on sales spikes during key periods.
    2. Leverage High-Performing Store Strategies: Investigate successful strategies used by top-selling stores and apply them across lower-performing locations.
    3. Season-Specific Promotions: Develop targeted campaigns for winter and fall seasons to align with natural shopping trends.
    4. Investigate Additional Drivers: Analyze factors such as store size, demographics, and location to understand and replicate top performance drivers.
