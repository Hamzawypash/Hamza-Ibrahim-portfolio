About Me :

I am a results-driven Data Analyst with a passion for transforming data into actionable insights. Equipped with training from Generation UK’s Data Analytics program, I possess expertise in SQL, Python, Power BI, and Excel, enabling me to deliver impactful solutions to complex problems.

Throughout my journey, I have successfully utilized tools like Generative AI and advanced Excel techniques to analyze diverse datasets, uncovering trends such as seasonal revenue patterns and market-specific product demands. By crafting insightful visualizations and communicating findings effectively, I’ve demonstrated my ability to bridge the gap between technical analysis and strategic decision-making.

With a background in Electrical Engineering and hands-on experience with projects involving statistical analysis, data visualization, and workflow optimization, I thrive in collaborative environments. My technical skillset includes data cleaning and transformation (Python: pandas, NumPy), querying databases (SQL), and creating interactive dashboards (Power BI).

Beyond my technical expertise, I pride myself on problem-solving, adaptability, and attention to detail. Fluent in both Arabic and English, I bring strong communication skills to work effectively with diverse teams and stakeholders.

Outside of work, I am passionate about personal fitness, the stock market, and giving back to my community through volunteer work. I am driven to apply my analytical and interpersonal skills to projects that make a tangible impact and contribute to data-driven decision-making in a dynamic organization.
